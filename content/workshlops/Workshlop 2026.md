The **3rd SciQLop Workshlop** takes place on **September 15–17, 2026** at Jussieu campus, Paris. This is an **in-person only** event, limited to **50 participants**.

## Program

| Day | Content |
|-----|---------|
| Tuesday Sept 15 (½ day) | SciQLop overview and new features |
| Wednesday Sept 16 (full day) | Science presentations — results with SciQLop, and studies that could benefit from it |
| Thursday Sept 17 (full day) | Hands-on sessions — tutorials and real use cases |

A **conference dinner** will be held on Wednesday evening.
